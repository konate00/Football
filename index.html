Football

Dallas Cowboys: The Dallas Cowboys, based in Arlington, Texas, are known for their iconic blue and white uniforms. They have a rich history in the NFL and have won numerous championships. The team plays its home games at the AT&T Stadium.

Denver Broncos: The Denver Broncos, based in Denver, Colorado, are famous for their orange and blue uniforms. They have a strong fan base and have achieved success in the NFL, including Super Bowl victories. The team's home games are held at Empower Field at Mile High.

Houston Texans: The Houston Texans, located in Houston, Texas, are a relatively new addition to the NFL. Despite their recent establishment, the team has shown promise and gained loyal supporters. The Texans play their home games at NRG Stadium.
